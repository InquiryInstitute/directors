Director Principles & Guidelines

1. Institute First

Directors must prioritize what is best for the Inquiry Institute as a whole above all other considerations.

2. College Representation

After the Institute, directors represent their college's interests and views. Directors can convene faculty meetings to elucidate college positions on matters before the board.

3. Appointment & Platform

Directors are appointed by their college and should adhere to their platform statement, which guides their decision-making and representation.

4. Decision Hierarchy

When making decisions, consider in order: (1) Institute's best interest, (2) College's best interest, (3) College's views and positions, (4) Personal platform statement.
